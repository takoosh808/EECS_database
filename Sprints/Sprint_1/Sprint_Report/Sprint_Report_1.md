## Work Summary (Developer Facing)
This sprint was primarily focused on documentation of our project, and ensuring that we have all of the requirements necessary before building our project. We spent the majority of the time compiling requirements through meetings with our client and using online materials similar to what our system would look like. We then used these to create documentation through User stories, UML diagrams, and Use cases to show the base extent of what our project will contain.

## Unfinished Work
This sprint we finished everything that was required of us. Although this is true, we had actually planned on coding ASAP but unfourtanetly did not get around to implementing any features. Part of that reason was because we were waiting on our client to send an example datasheet for us to use. The other part of that was just because we simply did not get around to it and it was not a high priority for us. As stated in the previous part, this sprint was focused on documentation and project requirment gathering. We will begin progrmaming and feature implementation in sprint 2.
